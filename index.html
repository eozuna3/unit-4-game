Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of each game, as noted below


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value fo each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time you when the game and restart, the game will select a new number, and the values of each crystal will change as well.
